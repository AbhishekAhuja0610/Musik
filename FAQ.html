How does this music success prediction website work?

The app works by analyzing a range of data points or features such as streaming numbers, loudness, danceability and acousticness . These data points are fed into machine learning algorithms that use historical data to predict the likelihood of success for a new music release.

How much accuracy does it have?

It is 80%. The accuracy depends on the quality and quantity of data available. The more data available, the more accurate the predictions are likely to be. However, no prediction is 100% accurate, and there are many factors that can influence the success of a music release.
